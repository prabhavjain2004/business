Frequently Asked Questions

What is TapNex?

TapNex is a secure NFC-based payment and access control system that allows for contactless transactions and identification using NFC cards.

How do I get a TapNex card?

TapNex cards are issued by authorized administrators. Please contact your organization's administrator or visit our office to get a new card issued.

How do I top up my TapNex card?

You can top up your TapNex card at any authorized outlet or through an administrator. Simply present your card and make a payment using cash or UPI.

What if I lose my TapNex card?

If you lose your card, report it immediately to your administrator or contact our support team. We can deactivate the lost card and issue a replacement.

Is my information secure?

Yes, TapNex uses advanced encryption and security measures to protect your personal information and transaction data. Your card is also protected with a secure key.

Troubleshooting

Card Not Reading

If your card is not being detected by the reader, try the following:

  • Make sure you're holding the card close enough to the reader
  • Hold the card steady for at least 2 seconds
  • Ensure there are no other NFC cards or devices nearby
  • Check if the reader device has NFC enabled
  • If using a mobile device, ensure it supports NFC and has it enabled

Payment Failed

If a payment transaction fails, check the following:

  • Ensure your card has sufficient balance
  • Try scanning the card again
  • Check if the outlet's device is properly connected to the internet
  • Contact the outlet administrator for assistance

Browser Compatibility

For the best experience with TapNex:

  • Use Google Chrome on Android (version 89 or later)
  • Ensure NFC is enabled on your device
  • Allow browser permissions for NFC when prompted
  • Keep your browser updated to the latest version
